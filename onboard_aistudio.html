Getting Started

Now that you've downloaded the app and created your account, follow these simple steps:

  1. Complete Your Profile & Financials Verify your account and add financial information to get an accurate estimate of your mortgage affordability.
  2. Add Financial Assets to Maximize Affordability Listing all your assets will increase the total mortgage amount you can qualify for. Make sure to include as much as possible for the best results.
  3. Add Income to Refine Your Affordability Estimate Your income plays a key role in determining how much of the mortgage you can afford. Enter this information to receive tailored results.
    Note: Adding liabilities is optional and useful for understanding their impact on affordability. However, liabilities are not required for submitting an application, as we'll retrieve them during the credit check.
  4. Submit an Application Once your profile and financial details are complete, you can:
    • Create applications to see your options.
    • Try mock applications or invite friends and family to collaborate and explore joint mortgage possibilities.
    When you're ready, Approveage will guide you through a straightforward six-step to secure your home.
